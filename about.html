"浮世"- (n.) Floating World

Inspired by the Ukiyo-e Japanese art movement that flourished during the 17th and 19th century, Ukiyo is an athleisure wear brand that promotes the urban & pleasure-seeking lifestyle of the Japanese floating world culture of play and entertainment. Ukiyo bridges the gap between comfort and fashion, offering functionality, flexibility and style through the innovation of bamboo fabric infused with spandex.
